
夜
随着风涌动在无尽的黑
我的心
随着你的脚步
终于休止于有限的轮回

还想着谁
在等着谁
没有谁
值得我千杯不醉
——
吞下这苦酒
然后被吞没于昏黄的伤悲

不敢
再看你落下眼泪
我的世界
就是将你的背影追随
所以走向哪里
已然无所谓
也无论——
你的错与对
我的是与非


Night
With the wind surging in the endless darkness
My heart
With your footsteps
Finally resting in a finite cycle

Still thinking about who
Waiting for someone
No one
Worth a thousand drinks
---
Swallowing this bitter wine
And be swallowed up in a dusky sadness

I dare not
To see you shed tears again
My world
Is to follow your back
So where to go
It doesn't matter anymore
And no matter...
Your wrong or right
